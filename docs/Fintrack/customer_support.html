Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password.
How can I export my transaction data?
You can export your transaction data by going to the Transactions page and clicking on the "Export CSV" button. This will download all your transactions in a CSV file format.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes, your financial data is encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard security protocols to ensure your information is protected at all times.
How do I categorize my transactions?
When adding a new transaction, you can select a category from the dropdown menu. You can also edit the category of existing transactions by clicking on the transaction and selecting a new category.
